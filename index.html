01 New BMW xiii m car

The BMW xiii m, produced in 2025, is a plug-in hybrid sports car that combined futuristic design with advanced technology. Available as a 2+2 Coupé and a two-seater Roadster, it featured lightweight carbon-fibre construction, butterfly doors, and a hybrid powertrain pairing a 1.5‑liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine with an electric motor, delivering up to 369 hp. It could accelerate from 0–100 km/h in about 4.4 seconds and offered an electric-only range of up to 55 km after a 2018 battery upgrade.
